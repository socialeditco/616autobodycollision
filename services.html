Services

Auto body services with a clean repair path.

Each service section gives customers enough information to take the next step without overwhelming them.

Collision Repair

Structural thinking, clean panels, accurate documentation.

Collision repair starts with a clear inspection and a realistic plan. This section is designed to help customers understand that the shop can coordinate damage review, parts planning, panel work, bumper repair, and insurance estimate support.

  • Estimated turnaround: 3–10 business days depending on parts, insurance approvals, and repair complexity.
  • Ideal for bumper damage, side impacts, fender repairs, panel replacement, and accident restoration.
  • Quote approach: photo review first, then in-person inspection when needed.

Painting

Color-match refinishing with a premium final presentation.

Paint work should feel precise, not rushed. This demo frames painting as a high-detail service covering surface prep, color matching, refinishing, clear coat restoration, and final polish.

  • Estimated turnaround: 2–7 business days based on panel count, prep time, and curing requirements.
  • Ideal for repainted panels, scratch correction, clear coat issues, and post-collision refinishing.
  • Quote approach: photos help start the estimate, but paint matching may require an in-shop review.

Dent Repair

Dings, dents, and panel cleanup without the confusion.

Dent repair pages work best when the customer can quickly determine whether their issue is minor cosmetic damage, paint-impact damage, or a larger collision-related repair.

  • Estimated turnaround: same day to 3 business days depending on severity and paint damage.
  • Ideal for door dings, shallow dents, hail-style marks, lease returns, and pre-sale touchups.
  • Quote approach: clear photos from multiple angles usually help speed up the estimate.
