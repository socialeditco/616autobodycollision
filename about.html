Shop History

Built around straight answers and clean repairs.

616 Autobody & Collision is presented here as a locally focused repair shop serving Grand Rapids and the surrounding West Michigan market. The brand voice is direct: inspect the damage, explain the options, help with the next step, and deliver a repair customers feel good about.

The visual system keeps the experience dark, premium, and confident while the content stays practical for real customers dealing with inconvenient vehicle damage.
