How It Works

Fast estimate intake. Clear next step. No pressure.

Customers can submit repair details, choose the type of service they need, and request a preferred appointment time in one clean two-column layout.

For a real launch, these form actions could connect to email, a CRM, Google Calendar, or an estimate workflow. For this proposal, every action remains local and safe.

Client customization note:

These quote and appointment fields can be customized for the shop’s real workflow, including image uploads, VIN, license plate, insurance claim number, preferred communication method, vehicle drop-off needs, and any other details you want customers to provide up front.

We can also add a backend if preferred, giving the owner or staff a simple place to review submissions and add, remove, or manage gallery images without touching the website code.
